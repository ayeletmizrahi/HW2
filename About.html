About Me

I am Gal Friedman Malka, married to Elad. In the last five and a half years, I managed a dental clinic in Kiryat Tivon.
In the past year, I decided to embark on a new path.
I took courses, attended workshops, read numerous books in the spiritual world,
and today, I am a numerologist and a coach for couples promoting relationships.

The spiritual world has always fascinated me since I was a little girl, asking myself and my family questions about life after death. I experienced many losses at a young age and always knew that the departed could hear me and watch over me.
I always knew that there is something greater than us that guides us and directs our path.
For many years, I felt lost, not truly understanding what I wanted to do with myself until almost two years ago when I almost lost my father.

I began to look at the world and my life differently, distancing myself from a job I registered for only out of necessity, not genuine interest.
I found numerology and discovered a whole new world.
I found a way to connect with myself and discovered my purpose.
The moment I learned numerology, I understood that I had to pass on this knowledge.
It's not something that can stay with me alone.
